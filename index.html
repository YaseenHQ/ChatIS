yChat (a fork of IS2511's ChatIS) is a sleek chat overlay that brings your Twitch chat to life.
It Supports BTTV, FFZ, and 7TV emotes with auto-refresh.
Customize it with animations, chat fading, bot filters, and custom fonts.
Works with OBS Studio, Meld Studio, IRLtoolkit or any software with a Browser Source option.

  • FFZ, BTTV, 7TV emotes & badges
  • 7TV username paints
  • Animations, shadows, fading
  • Bot filtering
  • Custom fonts
